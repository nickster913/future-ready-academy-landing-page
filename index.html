Common questions

What parents ask us

Does my child need prior robotics experience?
No. We start from fundamentals on Day 1 and progress to the certification assessment by Day 2. Curious and willing is all they need.
What exactly is the Carnegie Mellon certification?
An official Carnegie Mellon Robotics Academy certification verifying competency in coding and computational thinking with VEX IQ. Future Ready Academy is the only CMRA-authorised partner in Singapore — your child's certificate comes directly from CMU.
How does this help with DSA applications?
DSA panels at SST, Hwa Chong, and Raffles verify credentials directly. A CMU certification plus a VEX IQ record is one of the strongest portfolio items for Computing and Robotics DSA tracks.
What happens after the bootcamp?
Graduates can continue on the full robotics programme toward VEX IQ competitions and higher CMU certifications. Every seat includes a free pathway consultation.
Why only 12 seats?
Hands-on assessment requires small groups. Every child gets direct instructor attention and a meaningful certification — not a participation certificate handed to 50 kids in a hall.
